The Metropolitan Museum of Art

Explore 5,000 years of art and culture from around the world — experience creativity, history, and inspiration at The Met.

The Met’s collection includes over 2 million works of art ranging from ancient artifacts to contemporary pieces. Explore paintings, sculptures, textiles, armor, and more from cultures around the globe.

Our galleries are organized by departments including European Paintings, Asian Art, Egyptian Art, and more, offering something for every art enthusiast.
