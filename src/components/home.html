The Metropolitan Museum of Art

Explore 5,000 years of art and culture from around the world — experience creativity, history, and inspiration at The Met.

Visit Us

The Metropolitan Museum of Art presents over 5,000 years of art from around the world for everyone to experience and enjoy. The Museum lives in two iconic sites in New York City—The Met Fifth Avenue and The Met Cloisters. Millions of people also take part in The Met experience online.

Plan your visit and explore our galleries, exhibitions, and online resources designed to connect you with art from every era and culture.

What We Have

The Met’s collection includes over 2 million works of art ranging from ancient artifacts to contemporary pieces. Explore paintings, sculptures, textiles, armor, and more from cultures around the globe.

Our galleries are organized by departments including European Paintings, Asian Art, Egyptian Art, and more, offering something for every art enthusiast.
